
# Rave Cave - Brand Design & Experience Intent (BDEI)
**Version:** 3.4.3  
**Last Updated:** February 3, 2026  
**Creative Direction:** Jodie (R/GA)

---

## 1. Brand Essence
**Name Origin:** "Rave Cave"
- **Cave:** Traditional wine cellar, sophistication.
- **Rave:** Modern, vibrant, tech-forward.

## 2. Visual Aesthetic
**Industrial Tech-Sommelier**
- Exposed concrete backgrounds.
- Neon violet/green accents against neutral tones.
- High-contrast typography (Inter + JetBrains Mono).

## 3. Color Palette
- Background Dark: #1a1a1a
- Neon Pink: #FF006E
- Acid Green: #CCFF00
